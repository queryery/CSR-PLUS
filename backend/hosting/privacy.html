Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 July 2026

This policy explains what data the CSR+ browser extension and its backend collect, why, and how it is handled. We aim to collect as little as possible.

What we collect

  • Discord identity — when you sign in for paid features, we store your Discord user ID, username and avatar reference. This is used to identify your account and apply your tier and cosmetics.
  • Cosmetic content — banner images or clips you choose to publish. These are stored so they can be shown to other CSR+ users, and are screened by automated content moderation (Google Cloud Vision) plus manual review.
  • Reports — if you submit a player report, we store the reason, an optional description, the target and reporter IDs, and any screenshot or clip you attach, so a moderator can review it.
  • Basic usage signals — anonymous-ish activity counts (such as daily active pings and the extension version) used to understand usage and support the Service.

What we do NOT collect

CSR+ is free and takes no payments, so we do not collect any payment, card or billing information. There is no checkout and no payment processor involved.

How data is used

We use the data only to operate the Service: authenticate you, apply your cosmetics, show published content to other CSR+ users, moderate content, process reports, and keep the Service working. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

Storage & providers

Data is stored using Google Firebase (Firestore, Cloud Storage and Cloud Functions) hosted in the EU region. Content moderation uses Google Cloud Vision. Report notifications may be delivered to our private moderation channels (Telegram and/or Discord) for review.

Retention

Account records are kept while your account exists. Published cosmetic content is stored until you replace or remove it or the account is closed. You can request deletion of your data by contacting us.

Your choices

You can sign out at any time, and request access to or deletion of your data by emailing us. Uninstalling the extension stops all local activity signals.
