Map veto

// 04

Drag to set your ban order. Top is banned first; the bottom map is the one you keep.

Ban priority
    With auto-ban on, CSR+ bans your two lowest-priority maps each turn. In the final round only two maps remain and the site runs a single-map vote, so just one is banned.
