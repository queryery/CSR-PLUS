CSR+ is a browser extension that layers player insights, match automation and profile customization on top of the CS:Restored web client — so you spend less time clicking and more time playing.

What it does

Everything below is free. Pro & Premium add shared cosmetics on top.

Player insight badges

Form, consistency and skill tiers on every player in the lobby and match room, computed from recent games.

Win-probability bar

A live estimate of each team's chances, updated as the match room fills.

Queue automation

Optional auto-accept, auto-invite friends and map-ban priority so you never miss a pop.

CSR+ Studio

Customize your accent colors, name style, card flair and avatar frame — previewed live before you publish.

Player reports

Report cheating or abuse with a screenshot or clip; reports reach a human moderator queue.

Trades & docs

Quick trade links with confirmed friends and an in-extension manual for every feature.

Install CSR+

CSR+ is free to use. Shared cosmetics require a quick Discord sign-in inside the extension (so your banner belongs to you and can be moderated) — but nothing is ever charged.

Questions

The short version.

Who sees my banner and cosmetics? Every player who also has CSR+ installed — on your match-room card, lobby slot and profile. Players without CSR+ see the normal site.
Do I have to pay for anything? No. CSR+ is completely free and there are no subscriptions. Shared cosmetics just need a Discord sign-in so your uploads belong to you and can be moderated.
What data do you collect? As little as possible — your Discord identity for sign-in, any cosmetic content you publish, and reports you submit. See the Privacy Policy.
Is CSR+ affiliated with CS:Restored? No. CSR+ is an independent, third-party enhancement for the CS:Restored web client and is not endorsed by or affiliated with it.
