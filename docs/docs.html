CSR+ — Enhanced Match Suite

Everything the extension does, how it works, and how to control it. CSR+ runs on csrestored.fun and adds match intel, automation and quality-of-life tools on top of the site — nothing is modified server-side, and none of it gives gameplay advantages.

01Getting started

Install the extension, then open csrestored.fun and sign in as usual. CSR+ activates automatically on the site. Click the CSR+ icon in your browser toolbar to open the control popup — every feature described below can be toggled there.

Tip: most data features need you to be signed in on the site, because CSR+ reads the same API your own browser session uses (match history, friends, inventory).

02Master switch

The big switch at the top of the popup's General tab turns the whole extension on or off. When off, every injected element (badges, bars, buttons, overlays) is removed from the page instantly and all automation loops stop. Nothing else needs to be un-configured.

03The popup

The popup is CSR+'s control console. Its tabs:

TabWhat it controls
GeneralMaster switch, player badges, win probability and other overlays.
AutomationAuto-accept (with countdown delay) and smart map veto.
AppearanceTheme (Black / White) and the stats period used for badges (today / yesterday / last 10).
Map vetoDrag your ban order. Top of the list is banned first; the bottom map is the one you want to keep.
FriendsWhose party invites are auto-accepted, plus the ⇄ shortcut to send a trade offer.
SoundUI and match sound cues, per-group toggles, volume, previews.
FeedbackBug reports / ideas for the beta features.
AboutVersion, update check, changelog, credits, and this manual.

The sidebar also shows the community live users counter and a DOCS shortcut to this page.

04Player badges & rating

In the match room every player card gets a strength badge (e.g. Strong, Average) computed from their ELO and recent performance:

  • Elo-led hybrid model: ELO dominates the score; K/D, kills-per-round, estimated ADR, winrate and current form add up to a smaller performance nudge.
  • Recency-weighted: newer matches count more than old ones.
  • Confidence-weighted: players with very few games are pulled toward "Average" on purpose — 3 games is not enough evidence to call someone a demon or a bot.

Hover a badge for the full tooltip: sample size, rating, K/D, K/R, winrate, form trend, win/loss streak and K/D stability. The time range (today / yesterday / last 10 games) is set in Appearance.

Note: the site's API has no per-round damage, so ADR is an estimate: K/R × 100 + assists/round × 22, clamped to a sane range.

05Win probability

A blue/red bar above the two teams in the match room showing each side's chance to win. It uses the FACEIT-style logistic curve on the teams' average ELO (1 / (1 + 10^((eloB − eloA)/400))) with a small per-player performance adjustment. The bar fills in as player stats stream in, and always aligns itself to the team grid — it stays clear of the match room chat sidebar.

06Match Found overlay

When a match is found, CSR+ rebuilds the confirm window with a full intel panel: every player's avatar, name, tag, strength badge, K/D, K/R, ADR and winrate, sorted by strength, plus the win-probability bar. Rows fill in progressively as stats arrive, so the panel appears instantly.

The auto-accept countdown lives in this panel's header (or floats top-right if the window hasn't rendered yet). You can always click Accept manually — CSR+ forwards the click to the site's real button.

07Notes & tags

Click any player card in a match room to open a compact popover with their stats and two private fields:

  • Tag — a short label (e.g. smurf, mate, toxic) shown as a chip on their card every time you meet them again.
  • Note — free text only you can see.

Everything is stored locally in your browser. Nothing is uploaded anywhere.

Group / lobby cards: clicking a lobby member opens their CSR+ profile. Site controls on the card — like the kick ✕ — still work normally; CSR+ ignores clicks on them.

08Auto-accept

Automatically accepts the match when one is found. Configure in Automation:

  • Delay — a countdown (with ticking sound, if enabled) before the accept is clicked, so you have a moment to cancel.
  • Works together with the Match Found overlay; the countdown is shown in its header.

09Smart map veto

Bans maps for you according to your priority list (drag to reorder in the Map veto tab). The veto on the site is turn-based; CSR+ reads whose turn it is and how many bans the turn allows, then bans your least-wanted maps first. In the final vote round (two maps left, each player votes for one) it casts exactly one vote for your preferred remaining map.

Heads-up: the bottom map of your list is the one CSR+ tries to keep alive.

10Server copy

When your match server is assigned, CSR+ arms the connect line so a single click copies a ready connect ip:port command to your clipboard — even when the site displays the address as "Hidden", the real address from the join link is used. A toast confirms the copy.

11Friend invite filter

In the popup's Friends tab, select which friends' party invites should be auto-accepted. With nobody selected, every invite is accepted. Selected friends are marked with a ✓.

12Profile pages

CSR+ has its own profile page (opened from player cards, popovers, or lobby clicks). It mirrors the site profile — header with avatar, flag and Steam / SteamDB / FaceitFinder links, a statistics grid (winrate, ELO, KDR, AVG, rating, kills, deaths, matches, wins) and a full match history — and adds Overview / History / Inventory tabs.

13Inventory viewer

On any user page a ▦ Watch inventory button opens that player's inventory in the CSR+ viewer: item icons, rarity colours, float & wear (FN/MW/FT/WW/BS), pattern seed, StatTrak™ counts, name tags, and Doppler-phase / Case-Hardened gem badges (data by smel). Sort and filter by type, rarity or name.

14CSR+ Trades beta

A full replacement for the site's trade screen. Open it from the site's Trades link (CSR+ offers to take over — your choice is remembered) or from the popup.

  • Trades list — all incoming/outgoing offers with search, direction and date filters, item previews and coin amounts.
  • Create offer — pick a friend, select items from both inventories side by side, attach coins on either side, review the summary and send.
  • Item cards show rarity, float, wear, seed, StatTrak™ and name tags — details the default page hides.

15Send trade button new

On the site's friends page, every confirmed friend row gets a ⇄ Send trade button that opens CSR+ Trades with that friend already selected and their inventory loading. Pending and incoming friend requests deliberately get no button — only mutual friends can be traded with. The same shortcut (⇄) appears next to each friend in the popup's Friends tab.

16CSR+ Cases

A faster, lag-free case-opening screen that replaces the site's cases page while active. The site's own page underneath is paused so your machine isn't rendering two case grids at once.

17Leaderboard search

Adds a search box to the leaderboard so you can find any player by name without paging. Players outside the current page are looked up through the API and shown with their rank and stats.

18ELO tracker

Tracks your ELO across sessions and shows your gains/losses, so you can see your progression at a glance without doing mental math after every match.

19Profile customization Free

Make your profile and player card yours: a custom banner image behind your profile header, accent colours, name styles and card flair — visible to every other CSR+ user who views your profile or meets you in a match.

  • Banner — upload your own image; it is moderated automatically (and rejected if it contains restricted content) before anyone else can see it.
  • Colours & styles — accent colour, name gradient and card border flair.
  • Sync — stored in the cloud and applied wherever CSR+ renders you.

Customization is completely free. Sign in with Discord in the customization editor (popup → Profile+) to share your look — banners and animated cosmetics — with other CSR+ players. Sign-in just ties your uploads to your account so they can be moderated; nothing is ever charged.

Moderation: banners pass an automated safety scan before publishing. Repeated attempts to publish restricted content can permanently disable customization for the account.

20Themes

Two popup themes, both monochrome: Black (neon-white on deep black, default) and White (ink on bright concrete). Switch in Appearance. Team colours and rarity colours stay constant across themes so data always reads the same.

21Sounds

Small audio cues for hover/click, toggles, match found, the accept countdown ticks and accept/cancel. The Sound tab has a master toggle, volume slider, per-group switches and preview buttons. Browsers only allow audio after your first interaction with the page — that's normal.

22Updates

With Auto-update check on, CSR+ checks GitHub for a new release when you open the popup (at most once an hour) and shows a modal when one is available. Check for updates forces a check. Updating opens the GitHub release page — install the new build from there.

23Privacy & safety

  • Notes, tags and all settings are stored locally in your browser.
  • CSR+ talks to the same site API your session already uses; it does not collect analytics.
  • The live users counter only increments an anonymous public counter — no identifying data.
  • Profile customization (opt-in) stores only what you publish: banner, colours and your Discord ID.
  • No gameplay advantages: everything CSR+ does is visible information and UI automation.

Full policy: PRIVACY.md.

24FAQ

Badges say "Average" for a player who is obviously smurfing.

If they have very few games, the confidence weighting pulls them toward Average on purpose. Check the tooltip's Sample row.

Nothing shows up on the site.

Check the master switch, make sure you're signed in on csrestored.fun, and reload the tab.

Auto-copy of the connect command didn't fire.

Browsers require a click before allowing clipboard writes. Click the connect line once — it's always armed.

The kick ✕ in my group opens a profile instead of kicking.

Fixed in 0.0.9 — CSR+ now passes clicks on site controls straight through.
